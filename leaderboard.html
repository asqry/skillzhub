SkillzHub Partner Manager Leaderboard

The SkillzHub Partner Manager Leaderboard is where can record how many partners they get, and make acquiring partners a fun competition. Each season lasts four weeks, and the partner manager that gets the most amount of partners in the season will get a star next to their name for every season that they win. If you acquire a partner, press the button below and copy-paste the partner message from the partnered server in the box.
